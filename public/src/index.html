Open measurement and feedback tools for human interaction

We're building new research tools to measure and augment human interaction in real-time, leading to better group decision-making, trust, and engagement.

Open Badges

Wearable sensors for the social world.

Rhythm Open Badges is an open-source software/hardware project that aims to make it easier for researchers everywhere to quantitatively measure social interactions.

The open-source hardware badges can be worn by participants around their necks or clipped to their clothing, and measures speaking activity and proximity to other badges.

Breakout

Instrumenting online communication.

Rhythm Breakout is a software platform for collecting and analyzing online team communication. Using Breakout, researchers and organizations can better understand the nature of group intelligence and organizational structure.

Breakout is a modular, open-source system. It creates a standardized API for social communication data, and can be used as a real-time back-end for social interaction studies in person or on the web.
